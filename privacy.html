🙏 Introduction

Jai Shree Radha Raman Ji! Welcome to the official digital seva website of Shree Radha Raman Ji Vrindavan (accessible at vaishnavadiii786-ctrl.github.io/shree-vrindavan).

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using this website, you agree to the practices described here.

Ham aapki privacy ka poora samman karte hain. Yeh website purely devotional purpose ke liye banayi gayi hai — Shree Radha Raman Ji ke darshan, aarti timings, aur bhakti content ke liye.

02
📋 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Automatically Collected Data: IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the site (via Google Analytics).
  • Cookies & Local Storage: We use browser cookies and localStorage to save your preferences like Japa Mala count, prayer wall entries, and Virtual Diya status — all stored locally on your device only.
  • Prayer Wall Submissions: If you submit a prayer, your name, city, and prayer text are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data is NOT sent to any server.
  • Photos for Frames: If you use the Photo Frame feature, your photo is processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded to any server.
03
🍪 Cookies & Tracking

We use the following cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): To understand how visitors use our website. This uses cookies to track anonymous usage data. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out.
  • Functional Cookies: Used to remember your Japa Mala progress, Virtual Diya status, and prayer wall entries locally on your device.

You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

04
🔗 Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites including YouTube, Instagram, and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites.

  • YouTube & Instagram: These platforms have their own privacy policies.
  • WhatsApp: WhatsApp's privacy policy governs any interactions through our WhatsApp Channel.
05
⚖️ Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You can request information about data we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete: You can clear all locally stored data by clearing your browser's cookies and localStorage.
  • Right to Opt-out: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time.
  • Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your data by contacting us.

As this website stores most data locally on your device, you have full control — simply clear your browser data to remove all stored information.

06
👶 Children's Privacy

This website is open to all devotees including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. This website does not have registration, login, or any feature that collects personal information beyond what is described above.

All features (Japa Mala, Prayer Wall, Virtual Diya) store data locally on the user's own device and do not transmit personal information to any server.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
